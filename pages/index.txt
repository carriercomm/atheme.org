<p>
	We develop stuff, so check out the <a href="http://www.atheme.org/projects" title="Projects">projects page</a> for more information.
	For many development versions of the Atheme projects, check out <a href="http://github.com/atheme/" title="Git">our Git repository</a>.
</p>
<p>
	A new website is coming soon. Have any questions? Contact our webmaster via email, or <a href="http://webchat.staticbox.net/?channels=#atheme-project" title="#atheme-project on StaticBox IRC"><span class="italics bold">locate us on IRC via the webchat or at irc.atheme.org</span></a>.
	If you have an issue or a suggestion that you would like to "formally" report, open up an issue at <a href="http://github.com/atheme/atheme.org/issues/"><strong>at the bug tracker</strong></a> and it will be seen and taken care of promptly.
</p>
