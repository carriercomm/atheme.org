<ul class="headings">
	<li><h2>Welcome</h2></li>
	<li><h3>atheme.org consists of a wide assortment of activities covering a wide scope</h3></li>
</ul>

If you are looking for a rewarding experience with peer-directed meritocratically-driven projects, odds are
likely you will find it here.

<div class="row">
    <div class="span3">
        <h3>atheme.org is a strong, innovative community</h3>

        We consider ourselves, as members of atheme.org, a community of developers and users, driven to create high
        quality projects through unfettered innovation.
    </div>
    <div class="span3">
        <h3>atheme.org is a meritocracy</h3>

        Projects are driven by consensus-based processes designed to promote meritocratic decision making, with
        broadly usable licensing terms.
    </div>
    <div class="span3">
        <h3>atheme.org is leading innovation</h3>

        Members and activities that are part of atheme.org consistently lead innovation in the areas served by
        our activities.
    </div>
</div>
